Republic Records Executive: “The Weeknd’s Next Album Dropping Late Summer”

The Weeknd just had an album preview party at the Republic Records headquarters while being in New York for his concert event at the Museum of Modern Art. This was confirmed by several people that were there. Quite a bunch of Instagram pictures were posted by executives of the record label. Republic Records executive Big D has posted the following Instagram picture with caption “That album dropping late summer” which leads us to believe that the album will undergo the regular process and promotion of a normal release. Yes that means singles dropping, album art being announced, and possibly an album push-back – because let’s be honest, who drops an album in August?

The Weeknd – Where You Belong (Prod. Mike Dean)

The Fifty Shades Of Grey movie is right around the corner, set to hit theaters on February 13th. With it comes The Weeknd’s second track, “Where You Belong,” with the other being “Earned It” – released a little while back. “Where You Belong” features Mike Dean’s synth-heavy production and the usual array of powerful falsettos from the one and only Toronto-native R&B singer.
